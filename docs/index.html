Just The Temperature Privacy Policy

Introduction

This document will describe the kind of information we collect and how it is used.

What information we collect

We collect your postal code and ISO 3166-1 alpha-2 country code in order to get information about your local weather. This information is only gathered if you have granted the skill permissions to use your location in the alexa app. We do not intentionally retain any of the information about your location or local weathher.

Third Party Providers

We use the openweathermap.org to get your local weather. Your postal code and country code are sent to them in the clear. Please refer to openweathermap.org's privacy policy for more information about how they might use the information we send to them.

Policy Effective 04/26/2017
